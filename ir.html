INVESTOR RELATIONS

Building the next infrastructure layer.

AntaraLogic® invites strategic partners who believe that AI’s next chapter isn’t about smarter models — it’s about smarter groups.

The AI industry has spent a decade perfecting the conversation between one human and one machine. Nobody has built the infrastructure for what happens when groups think with AI.

AntaraLogic® is closing that gap. Through AntaraPlatforms®, we are building a platform where small circles of people — families, teams, business partners — collaborate with an AI that understands the group, not just the individual. Three integrated products. One shared Data Lake®. A constraint architecture capped at twelve members, designed to make trust computable.

We don’t compete with AI models. We give them the one thing they lack: group context.

The addressable market begins with ASEAN’s seventy million SMEs — businesses that run on group chats, gut instinct, and relationships built face-to-face. These are enterprises too small for Salesforce but too serious for WhatsApp. AntaraPlatforms® meets them where they already are: in conversation.

AntaraLogic® is a Malaysian-incorporated company with a clear vision, a validated architecture, and a founder with twenty years of enterprise technology experience across Asia-Pacific. We welcome conversations with partners who share our conviction that ASEAN’s next chapter in AI should be built from within, not imported.
