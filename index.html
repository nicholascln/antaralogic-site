OUR STORY

Born at the dinner table.

"The world's smartest AI still talks to one person at a time. But the most important decisions in life are made together."

After twenty years selling enterprise technology across Asia-Pacific, Nicholas Chin — the founder of AntaraLogic® — saw the same pattern: groups succeed or fail based on dynamics no software ever captured.

Nick founded AntaraLogic® in 2026 in Johor Bahru, Malaysia, to build what didn't exist: AI that earns trust by listening to groups, not flattering individuals.
