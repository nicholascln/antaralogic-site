Our Products

Three Doors. One Ecosystem. Built for Groups.

AntaraPlatforms delivers three interconnected products that serve the full arc of how teams communicate, remember, and operate. Each product stands alone. Together, they form an ecosystem where every conversation, every note, and every customer interaction feeds a shared intelligence layer—securely, privately, and in full compliance with Malaysia’s Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA 2010, amended 2024).

@Sage®

Group & Solo AI Chat

The world’s first AI designed not for individuals talking to a machine, but for groups thinking together with one. Sage sits inside circles of one to twelve members—families, teams, business partners—and develops contextual understanding across every participant. Unlike conventional chatbots that respond to whoever is typing, Sage observes the full dynamic of group interaction and surfaces insights only when two or more independent signals from different members converge. It does not interrupt. It does not flatter. It speaks when something real has emerged. Validated through a six-model stress test across Claude, GPT, Gemini, Grok, Perplexity, and DeepSeek, and confirmed by Anthropic’s own research into contextual AI behaviour, Sage represents a new category: AI that earns trust by listening before it speaks.

Notã®

Personal Knowledge Management

A personal knowledge workspace where thoughts, notes, documents, and references are captured, structured, and made searchable by AI. Notã is where individual thinking happens before it becomes group conversation. Meeting notes flow from Sage into Notã. Customer records cross-reference with Kitã. Personal reflections stay private. The result is a second brain that integrates seamlessly with the collaborative layer—ensuring that what you know individually enriches what your group knows collectively, without compromising what remains yours alone.

Kitã®

SME Operations & CRM

A lightweight operations layer built for the seventy million small and medium enterprises across ASEAN that have outgrown spreadsheets but cannot justify enterprise software. Kitã manages contacts, tracks customer interactions, and organises operational workflows—all self-updating from the conversations already happening in Sage and the notes already captured in Notã. No duplicate data entry. No import-export rituals. The business simply runs, and Kitã keeps the record. For the kopitiam operator tracking suppliers, the design studio managing client feedback, or the family logistics firm coordinating deliveries—Kitã is the operations backbone they never had to hire someone to maintain.

The Ecosystem

The three products share a unified data architecture. Conversations in Sage, notes in Notã, and records in Kitã feed a common observability layer that enables cross-product intelligence without duplicating effort. When a team member mentions a supplier issue in a Sage conversation and another logs a pricing change in Kitã, the system recognises the connection. When meeting notes in Notã reference a decision that affects a customer in Kitã, the record updates. The intelligence is not artificial—it is the group’s own intelligence, surfaced and structured.

All data is processed in compliance with Malaysia’s Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA 2010, as amended in 2024) and aligned with the National Guidelines on AI Governance & Ethics issued by the Malaysian government in September 2024. Personal data remains under user control. Cross-border data processing adheres to PDPA requirements. The platform is designed with Privacy-by-Design principles, ensuring that data protection is not an afterthought but an architectural foundation.

“Sage was validated through a rigorous six-model stress test—its behavioural patterns examined across Claude, GPT, Gemini, Grok, Perplexity, and DeepSeek. Anthropic’s own interpretability research into contextual AI behaviour independently confirmed the principles that Sage’s architecture embodies: that meaningful AI intelligence emerges not from isolated prompts, but from sustained, multi-participant context.”

What Makes This Different

Every major AI platform today—ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Grok—is architected for one human talking to one machine. Some have recently introduced group chat features, allowing multiple users to interact with an AI in a shared thread. But adding users to a chat is not the same as understanding a group. A group chat with AI is a convenience feature. Sage is an architecture—designed from first principles to observe, learn from, and serve the dynamics of human collaboration, not merely respond to whoever types next.

This is not incremental improvement. It is a different category. And AntaraPlatforms, built by a Malaysian-founded team for the ASEAN market, is the first to bring it to life.
